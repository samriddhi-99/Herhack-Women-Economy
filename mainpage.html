Our Motive

WIN-IT is a global fellowship programme raised for the empowerment of women in economical field.
Women in different ages desire to become independent and want to do something on their own. Just to motivate them and help them to move forward with this, WIN-IT has come up with different ideas and internship programmes for different age groups including students, college going students and the ones who have crossed all these stages of their life.
